About Us

"Dogs have a way of finding the people who need them, and filling an emptiness we didn't ever know we had." - Thom Jones

Even as students hailing from four completely separate corners of the globe, we understand the important role that the domesticated animal can play in a persons life. So much so that we decided to create an app that would aid pet-seekers in their quest for their purrfect companion, working to pair owners with their dream pet whilst offering those in need (both human and otherwise) much needed affection and time for bonding in a safe, comfortable environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

At PawPals we work to match those who want a dog but don't have the time or money to take care of one. We work alongside dog shelters so that dogs who need love and attention can go on walks and spend quality time with amazing people like you! So what are you waiting for, find yourself a PawPal and go make some memories!

If you look to the top right you will be able to click on the 'Sign Up' button (it's highlighted in blue!), just fill out the form and click the Register button and you're all set!

"If you feel your time at PawPals is at an end you are very welcome to delete your account (though we will be sad to see you leave!). If you navigate to the edit profile page (by clicking on the 'Edit Profile' button at the top right of any page) you have the option to delete your account. Friendly warning for Shelter Managers: deleting the account of your shelter will subsequently remove all PawPals from your shelter from our databases!"

If you find yourself a PawPal you want to walk; either by the 'Dog's That Need Love' section on the homepage or through either the Dog Search or Shelter pages, you can click the 'Request a Walk' button below the PawPal's image. Just leave a message for the shelter outlining your request and submit! The shelter will get back in touch with you about the walk at their earliest convinience!

To View/Manage your requests you first need to navigate to the 'Manage Requests' page, which you'll see at the top right of any page when you are logged in. For standard users you will be able to view the status of every request you've made and easily be able to leave a review if you've completed a walk with a certain PawPal from this page. For shelter manager users you will be able to view requests made for PawPals your shelter houses, and review them as you please - accepting or denying requests based on your availability.
